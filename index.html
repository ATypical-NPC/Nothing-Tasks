TASKS

Assume a task is done once its deadline passes, so you don’t have to open the app just to tick boxes. Only tasks you gave a time are ever assumed — anything without one still waits for you. Auto-marked tasks show an AUTO tag, and the evening Wrap-up lets you undo any you didn’t actually do.

Save a copy of your data as a file on your device, or restore from one. Nothing is uploaded anywhere.

SYNC — GOOGLE DRIVE

Keep this device and your phone in step. Tasks, workouts and settings are stored in a hidden app file in your own Google Drive — newest change wins. Music files stay on each device (move them with Export/Import library). One-time setup steps are in the Guide.
